UniBuddy

Do you find difficult to study in your first experience at University?
Do you find difficult in studying alone? Do you prefer to study in groups?

We have the solution to your problems: UniBuddy

UniBuddy is a platform in which students can organize groups to study the same subjects and confront themselves in the same problems. The platform was created as a site dedicated to the university students of the Politecnico to encourage an initial interaction between off-site students who may have difficulty in finding somebody to study and socialize with.

Groups have the possibility to find a place because they can book rooms in universities or use the multiple study rooms scattered around the city. In each group there will be the opportunity to have tutor that has a very good knowledge in that subject in order to assure a good study and provide help assuring that you'll learn thanks to the meeting.
